ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Richard Ocampo, and I am a junior frontend developer passionate about technology and software. With 18 years of age and 1 year of hands-on experience, I have been deeply involved in the great world of web development.

My skill set encompasses a wide array of technologies and tools. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which serve as the foundation of my work. Additionally, I have expertise in utilizing frameworks such as Bootstrap and React to streamline development processes and enhance project efficiency.

I am dedicated to delivering projects and continuously expanding my skills in the ever-evolving field of web development. One of the highlights of my experience has been my proficiency in WordPress development. I've leveraged the power of this versatile platform to build dynamic websites with customizable features and seamless user experiences.

As I continue to grow in my career, I am committed to staying abreast of emerging technologies and industry trends. I thrive on challenges and am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in web development.

In addition to my technical skills, I pride myself on my strong communication and collaboration abilities. I enjoy working closely with clients to understand their needs and translate their vision into reality.
